Learning Outcomes

  • Understand fundamental horse behaviors and communication.
  • Recognize essential safety equipment for riding.
  • Develop basic riding techniques to increase comfort in the saddle.
  • Implement groundwork to enhance rider-horse relationships.
  • Set achievable riding goals and strategies to manage fear.

Welcome to the "Confident Rider Basics" course! This course is designed specifically for beginner horse owners who wish to build confidence in their riding skills. We understand that starting your journey in the equestrian world can be daunting, but with the right knowledge and support, you can become a confident and capable rider.

Understanding Horse Behavior

Learn the fundamental aspects of how horses communicate and behave, and how to respond to their needs effectively.

Understanding Horse Behavior

This lesson covers the various ways horses express themselves and how you can read their body language. Understanding your horse is crucial to building a strong partnership.

  • How to interpret equine body language.

  • Common behaviors and what they mean.

  • Basic needs of horses.

Safety First: Riding Gear and Stable Management

Explore the essential safety equipment for riding and learn proper stable management techniques to ensure your horse's wellbeing.

Safety First: Riding Gear and Stable Management

Safety is paramount in equestrian activities. This lesson focuses on the necessary riding gear for you and your horse, along with stable management practices.

  • Types of riding gear.

  • Safety checks before riding.

  • Daily stable management practices.

Basic Riding Skills and Techniques

Gain practical riding skills that will help you become more comfortable in the saddle while fostering a connection with your horse.

Basic Riding Skills and Techniques

This lesson introduces you to the fundamental riding techniques. You will learn how to mount, hold the reins properly, and maintain balance.

  • Mounting and dismounting safely.

  • Basic rein handling.

  • Maintaining balance in the saddle.

Building Confidence Through Groundwork

Learn groundwork exercises that can help build confidence for both horse and rider.

Building Confidence Through Groundwork

Groundwork is an essential part of building a trusting relationship with your horse. This lesson covers basic groundwork exercises that enhance your confidence and your horse's responsiveness.

  • Introduction to groundwork exercises.

  • Establishing boundaries and respect.

  • Enhancing communication skills.

Setting Goals and Overcoming Fear

Discover techniques for setting realistic riding goals and strategies to manage and overcome fear while riding.

Setting Goals and Overcoming Fear

This lesson focuses on placing your riding experience in context and establishing achievable goals. We will also explore methods to combat fear related to riding.

  • Identifying personal riding goals.

  • Techniques for overcoming fear in the saddle.

  • The importance of mindset in riding.

Reflection / Quiz
