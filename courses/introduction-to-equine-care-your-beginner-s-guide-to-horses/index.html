Welcome to 'Introduction to Equine Care'! This comprehensive 6-week course is designed for horse enthusiasts and beginners who want to learn the essential skills and knowledge needed to care for horses effectively. Join us as we dive into the world of horses, exploring everything from their basic anatomy to proper grooming techniques, and discover how to build a loving and safe relationship with these magnificent creatures.

Week 1: Understanding Horse Anatomy

In this lesson, we'll explore the basic anatomy of horses, focusing on their unique structures and how these relate to their care.

The horse's anatomy includes key systems such as the muscular, skeletal, and cardiovascular systems. Understanding these will help you recognize signs of health and illness in your horse.

Reflection / Quiz

At the end of the course, you'll take a quiz to test your knowledge on horse care. The quiz will include questions about anatomy, grooming, and behavior.
